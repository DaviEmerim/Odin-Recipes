Out of ideas for lunch? Get some eggs and flour to prepare this very simple german dish

ingredients:

  • 2 beaten eggs
  • 1/2 tablespoon of salt
  • 2 and 1/2 teacups of flour
  • 3/4 teacup of water

Cooking Steps:

  1. Beat the eggs in a bowl; add the salt, flour and water to make a dough, constantly beating it
  2. Boil water in a pan, as if you were going to cook some pasta
  3. With a wet dessert spoon, get spoonful pieces of the dough and place them in the water
  4. When the little doughs doubles their size and start floating on the water's surface, drain the pan and serve the cless
  5. Add any sauces and condiments you like with it!

This dish can be combined with any sauces and condiments you want! The imagination is the limit :D
