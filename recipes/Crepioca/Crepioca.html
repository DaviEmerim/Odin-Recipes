This recipe is also known as the "brazilian pancake". Say you're really on the run and you need something quick and healthy, but you don't have any bread. Got some tapioca and eggs? You just have what you need for a good breakfast.

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 8 tbsp of tapioca
  • 2 tbsp of milk
  • Salt and pepper at taste
  • A little cooking oil
  • Ham and cheese slices at taste

Cooking steps:

  1. Mix the eggs, tapioca, salt and pepper in a mixer
  2. Pre-heat a frying pan with a little bit of oil
  3. Put all the mixture on the frying pan in the shape of a pancake
  4. Fry both sides untill they're cooked, place a couple cheese slices over the crepioca when cooking the other side, so it melts
  5. Put a slice of ham inside and serve it
