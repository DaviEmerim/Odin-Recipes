You feel like after lunch and dinner there's something missing to make your meal perfect? You're looking for a good dessert, and this tart absolutelly kills it!

Crust Ingredients:

  • 2 teacups of wheat flour
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 200g of saltless butter in cubes
  • 4 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder

Filling Ingredients:

  • 350ml of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of maize starch
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 400g oof condensed milk

Topping Ingredients:

  • 250ml of hot water
  • 25g of strawberry-flavoured gelatine powder
  • 250ml of cold water
  • 250g of sliced strawberries

Cooking Steps:

Crust:

  1. In a mixer, add the flour, yolks, butter, sugar, baking powder and beat it
  2. Distribute the crust in a baking pan with removable bottom
  3. Place the pan in a pre-heated oven at 180Â°C for 15min
  4. Remove from the oven and let it cool down

Filling:

  1. In a big cup, mix the milk and maize starch to pour it on a pan
  2. Mix in the condensed milk and the egg yolk untill it thickens

Topping:

  1. Boil a pan with water and add the gelatine powder
  2. Keep mixing it and add a little of cold water
  3. Let it rest for 2 hours

Assembly:

  1. Place the filling on the crust, add the sliced strawberries and cover it with the strawberry gelatine
  2. Take it to the refrigerator for 3 hours

And there you have it. Enjoy the meal!
