This is one of the those things you can't die before tasting it, and once you have, it becomes an addiction. Brazil's "Pão de Queijo" (or Cheese Bread) is one the countries most typical recipes. If you go to Macdonalds in Brazil, they serve it there Do you want a perfect afternoon? Make yourself some cheese breads and serve it with coffee. Follow this simple recipe

Ingredients:

  • 200g of cheese (any kind works, use the best for an amazing experience)
  • 500g of sour starch, the ingredient that does the magic
  • 1 carton of whipped cream
  • A bit of salt if you're using less salty cheeses
  • A little bit of cooking oil

Cooking steps:

  1. Grate de cheese into thick shreds
  2. Add the whipped cream and mix well, also add a little bit of salt if needed
  3. Add the sour starch little by little, mixing it with a spoon untill it becomes a homogeneous, slightly sticky dough
  4. Pre-heat the oven at 180°C
  5. Grease a baking tray with oil
  6. Make balls with the dough and spread them over the baking tray
  7. Bake the cheese breads for untill they double their size and they brown, it should take around 30min
  8. Enjoy!
